About the Consultancy

Providing advanced Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), aerodynamic design, and structural analysis services to the renewable energy and heavy engineering sectors. With a Ph.D. from IIT Delhi and a strong foundation in industrial R&D, I specialize in transforming complex physical phenomena into actionable, optimized engineering designs.

Whether you need high-fidelity simulations for decentralized wind turbine installations, automated custom solvers, or structural evaluations of composite materials, I deliver robust, cost-effective computational solutions.

Consulting Services

Wind Turbine Aerodynamics

Design and analysis of Horizontal Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT). Expertise in large-scale rotor simulations using MRF and AMI models, aerodynamic optimization, and high-density grid performance evaluations.

Advanced CFD Modeling

Comprehensive fluid dynamics consulting utilizing OpenFOAM and ANSYS Fluent. Specialized in turbulence modeling (SST k-omega, Spalart-Allmaras), multiphase flows, aeroacoustics, and active flow control.

Structural Mechanics & FEA

Structural integrity analysis including tower design limit state evaluations, gear pair load calculations, and custom Finite Element Analysis for layered anisotropic composite beams using Classical Laminate Theory.

Engineering Automation

Development of custom Python-based tools to automate complex engineering workflows, from procedural 3D STL geometry generation to automated mesh creation and post-processing utility scripts.

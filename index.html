Profile

About me

CFD and multiphysics simulation engineer with expertise in turbulence modeling, aeroacoustics, reacting flows, and industrial flow simulations. Currently designing aerodynamic blades for low-wind-speed horizontal-axis wind turbines at InSolare Energy.

Previously at Tata Steel, I developed an OpenFOAM-based blast furnace simulator (BlaSim) coupling mass, momentum, energy, and species transport for ironmaking process optimization — modelling phenomena like ore reduction, coke gasification, and gas flow through packed beds.

My PhD at IIT Delhi focused on RANS and aeroacoustic simulations of multi-element high-lift airfoils (slat–main–flap), achieving up to 78% lift improvement and 50% noise reduction through optimised geometry and shear-layer interactions.

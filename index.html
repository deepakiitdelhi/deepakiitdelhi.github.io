About Me

I am a Computational Fluid Dynamics researcher with profound expertise in turbulence modeling, specifically utilizing OpenFOAM 7 for complex aerodynamic simulations. I integrate Python for advanced engineering automation, spanning from 3D STL geometry generation to structural Finite Element Analysis (FEA), bridging the gap between aerodynamic performance and structural integrity. My work focuses heavily on optimizing Horizontal Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT), coupling fluid simulations with precise technical documentation through LaTeX to pioneer sustainable engineering solutions.
